What I can contribute to FAC

Who am I?

I'm Jack Bridger and I'm writing this from Tokyo.

I've been here for almost 6 months studying machine learning and web development.

There's a long story to how this happened.

I applied to FAC in 2014 and you could say I've been slowly learning to code ever since.

I've run Code at Uni for 5 years, got a masters in CS and even worked at Stack Overflow.

Why do I want to be part of FAC?

Community.

The best resources are available online for free. We can teach ourselves anything in the world from anywhere.

But we all know the lone wolf programmer in her bedroom is a myth.

To make change we need friends and colleagues who believe in the same ideals.

Everyone I've met from FAC has been incredible!

What can I bring to FAC?

I believe so strongly in programming education and I will do whatever I can to help others when they are struggling.

I fell at those hurdles for longer than most.

With the cohort I will happily share everything I've learned running startups and working in sales.

Mostly I hope I can bring friendship and passion for making change using all types of technology (including Machine Learning!)
