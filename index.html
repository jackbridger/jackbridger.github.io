Why FAC? Why me?

I first applied to FAC 4 years ago. Since then I've been on a varied journey in the world of Computer Science, startups and even working at Stack Overflow. I hope to contribute to the curiousity, kindness and entrepreneurial spirit of the FAC community.

Education has enabled me to do so many things I never thought possible growing up.

And since Code at Uni gradually snowballed from one-off event to standalone business over the course of five years education has captured my imagination.

This year I have been self studying Data Science through every resource available.

It's been incredible to study the best resources in the world for free.

But, I really miss working with smart, conscientious people.

I understand Dan Sofer had a similar experience with online education.

This, plus the fact every FAC alum I've met has been awesome is why I am now focusing on applying to Founders and Coders.

Because I would love the opportunity to learn and work with like-minded people who care about technology and more importantly care about what technology can enable.

I'm also excited to help others beyond the program.

Great community combined with free online knowledge is something the world needs more of. I hope to be part of one and I hope to help create one in the future.
